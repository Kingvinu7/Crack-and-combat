üéÆ HOW TO PLAY

üéØ GAME OVERVIEW

Crack and Combat is a multiplayer strategy game where you must outwit an AI Oracle through 5 challenging rounds. Only the smartest survive!

üèÜ HOW TO WIN

Win the most rounds to become the ultimate champion! Each round tests different skills:

  • Riddles: Answer brain-teasers correctly and quickly
  • AI Challenges: Solve complex scenarios with strategic thinking
  • Fast Tapper: Test your reflexes in rapid-fire challenges

‚ö° ROUND STRUCTURE

Each round consists of:

  • Phase 1: Everyone answers a riddle (45 seconds)
  • Phase 2: Non-winners face AI challenges or skill tests
  • Scoring: Round winners get points, others get challenges

üß† STRATEGY TIPS

  • Think quickly but carefully - speed and accuracy both matter
  • In AI challenges, provide detailed, thoughtful responses
  • Watch the timer - auto-submission happens at 0 seconds
  • Study other players' answers to learn their strategies

  • Ctrl+Enter: Submit challenge responses
  • Click/Tap: Fast tapper challenges
  • Type: All text inputs support real-time entry
